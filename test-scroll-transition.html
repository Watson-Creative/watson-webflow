Site Menu - Background transitions from transparent to colors (Mobile Only)

Section 1: Forest

The background starts with the forest color at the top of the page. The site menu starts transparent (mobile only).

Section 2: Transitioning to Glacial

As you scroll, the background smoothly transitions to glacial. The site menu becomes visible (mobile only).

Section 3: Moving to Light Green

Continue scrolling to see the transition to light green. The site menu follows the same color progression (mobile only).

Section 4: Reaching Off-White

The background transitions to off-white and text changes to its default color. Site menu also reaches off-white (mobile only).

Section 5: Staying Off-White

The background remains off-white for all content beyond the first viewport.

Section 6: Scroll Back Up

Scroll back to the top to see the colors transition in reverse! Site menu becomes transparent again (mobile only).

Responsive Behavior

Mobile (≤991px): Site menu background transitions from transparent to colors
Desktop (>991px): Site menu background remains unchanged (uses default CSS)
